### About 
The International Workshop on Spatial Graphs 2026 is an international workshop devoted to  recent developments in spatial graphs and related topics in low-dimensional topology.

The aim of the workshop is to promote the exchange of ideas among researchers from different countries and institutions, and to provide opportunities for discussion on current progress and future directions in the field.

Topics of interest include, but are not limited to:
- spatial graphs
- handlebody-knots
- knotted surfaces
- simplicial complexes
- application to molecular topology
