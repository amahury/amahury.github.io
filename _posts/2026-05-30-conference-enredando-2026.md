### Introduction 
ENREDANDO is the Ibero-American School of Networks and Complex Systems, which is part of the activities of the Latin American Conference on Complex Systems, LANET. In its 2026 edition, it will address network analysis and complex networks with applications in biological, social, and artificial systems.

The event will take place between July 26 and July 31, 2026, on the campus of the Federal University of Viçosa (UFV) , in Viçosa, Minas Gerais, Brazil. The event will be part of the UFV's centennial celebrations.

Registrations are now open!

### Important dates
- Registrations: Until June 30th (Tuesday)
- Submission of papers: Until June 15th (Monday)
- Request for assistance (accommodation and registration fees): Until May 31st (Sunday)
- Payment of the registration fee with a discount (30%): until June 1st (Monday)
- Payment of the registration fee full amount due by July 6th (Monday)
- Payment Link will be provided by email after registration.

### Registration fees
- Students and postdoctoral researchers with discount (30%): R$ 180.00 or USD 35
- Students and postdoctoral researchers full price: R $ 260.00 or USD 50
- Professors and Researchers with discount: R$ 360.00 or USD 70
- Professors and Researchers full price: R$ 520.00 or USD 100

### Financial aid and accommodation
The second form allows you to request a full or partial exemption from the registration fee, which will be granted upon review by the organizing committee. After completing your registration for the event, use the form below to request this assistance: https://forms.gle/MuU4fGtjHRpbqMsj6  

In this same form, you can also request accommodation. For more details on accommodation and travel options, please visit the Routes/Logistics section .

Accommodation consists of shared dormitories with shared rooms and bathrooms, with bunk beds. Bed linen and towels are not provided, and there are no lockers with keys. Participants must bring their own items. If you cannot meet these conditions, please do not request accommodation.

### Submission of papers
We invite all participants to submit their work to the event. We accept research at various stages of development, from initial ideas to completed papers.
- Languages: Submissions in Portuguese, Spanish, or English will be accepted.
- Formats: poster and oral presentation. (Submissions are automatically accepted for poster presentation).
- Oral Presentation: If you wish to apply for an oral presentation slot, use the "Additional Comments" field to highlight the relevance of your research (e.g., published or in-progress articles).

Source and more info: [https://enredando.giscbr.org/inscricoes](https://enredando.giscbr.org/inscricoes)
