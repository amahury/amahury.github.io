### General Information
TaB is a one-day meeting dedicated to mathematical approaches in biology, designed to bring together researchers from diverse subfields to explore shared concepts and theoretical principles underlying complex biological systems. The event features invited talks, early-career researcher chalk-talk presentations, and flip-chart sessions, all aimed at fostering meaningful exchange and new collaborative opportunities within the Swiss and international biological modelling community.
