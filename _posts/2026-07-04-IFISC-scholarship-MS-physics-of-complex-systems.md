The IFISC offers a limited number of fellowships. One of them is sponsored by the Sicomoro Foundation. Each fellowship covers tuition fee and, additionally for applicants who are non-residents in Mallorca, 7000€. Conditions for these grants can be found in the following link. You can apply to them using the form below.

IFISC Best Master Thesis Award recognizes the best Master’s Thesis in the Master in Physics of Complex Systems at UIB. The award includes a €500 prize and possible online publication of the thesis. It is open to students who defend their thesis during their first year in the program. Funded by the Sicomoro Foundation. Details and conditions can be found here.

CSIC offers JAE-INTRO grants open to those who have scored a minimum of 8/10 in the bachelor's degree. For more information: https://sede.csic.gob.es/tramites/programa-jae/jae-intro-2026

The Spanish Ministry of Education offers general grants as well as collaboration grants. Master students can apply to both of them.

Additional grants, awards and general aid for master degree estudies at the webpage of the UIB Center of Postgraduate Studies

Fellowship offered by Fundación Carolina. Call is closed.

Santander Open Academy offers several grants for UIB postgraduate studies. Details can be found here.

Other options can be found at European Funding Guide.

What do you need to apply?

✅ Master's program pre-enrollment.
✅ CV & Academic transcripts.
✅ Statement of interest.
✅ 2 reference letters.

One of the grants is sponsored by the Fundación Sicómoro (requires C1 Spanish proficiency).

Application and more details: [https://ifisc.uib-csic.es/en/master/fellowships-for-the-masters-degree/](https://ifisc.uib-csic.es/en/master/fellowships-for-the-masters-degree/)
